WHAT IS ENT?

Epsilon Nu Tau is a Co-Ed Professional Entrepreneurship Fraternity striving to create a brotherhood of entrpreneurial students working together to advance the world, their community, and themselves through innovation.

More info can be found at the National Fraternity Website.

WHO IS THETA?

Theta is the 8th chapter of ENT located at Purdue University in West Lafayette, Indiana.
We are comprised of 25 brothers and 9 alumni.
