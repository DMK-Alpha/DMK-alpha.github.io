Epsilon Nu Tau is a Co-Ed Professional Entrepreneurship Fraternity. We are a brotherhood of entrepreneurial individuals working together to advance ourselves, our communities,
and the world through innovation.

Who is Theta?



Theta was founded in Spring of 2014 by Jazna Stannard and Brandon Davis as the 8th chapter of Epsilon Nu Tau. We have been growing strong ever since and currently have 25 active brothers and 13 alumni.

Being such a young organization, we function very much like a startup. As with any startup, there have been bumps in the road and perhaps a few pivots, but we've learned and matured along the way. We're not you're typical professional fraternity and we're proud of that, because we value thinking outside the box and challenging the status quo.
