Epsilon Nu Tau is
a co-ed professional fraternity of enrepreneurially-minded individuals.

We welcome all majors and do not require members to pursue the entrepreneurship
certificate Purdue University offers.

Why Join ENT?

Brotherhood
Enter a close-knit family of like-minded students
who will always have your back

Growth
Grow both professionally and personally surrounded by a concentration of highly motivated, ambitious,
and intelligent individuals

Success
Do the impossible with a strong network of connections as well as support from the brotherhood, for when the going gets tough
