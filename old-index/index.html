What is ENT?

Epsilon Nu Tau is a Co-Ed Professional Entrepreneurship Fraternity striving to create a brotherhood

of entrepreneurial students working together to advance the world, their community, and themselves through innovation.


More info can be found at the National Fraternity Website

What is Theta?

The Theta chapter of ENT is the 8th chapter at Purdue University, in West Lafayette, Indiana.

We are comprised of 21 brothers and 5 alumni.


Epsilon Nu Tau was founded on a core set of values.

Theta works to develop these values by focusing on the following:

Brotherhood

We are a family of entrepreneurs supporting and learning from one another.

Ethics

As leaders, we must ensure that we operate fairly and sustainably.

Innovation

We must continuously innovate to improve our chapter, campus, business ventures, and our lives.

Salesmanship

We must continuously strive to improve our ability to communicate our ideas.

History

A long line of successful entrepreneurs.

  • April 18th, 2008

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In early 2008, Epsilon Nu Tau was created at the University of Dayton as a unique coed fraternal organization built upon the ideals of professionalism and entrepreneurship, with fellowship and community as its backbone.

  • Spring 2014

    The Birth of Theta

    The Purdue chapter of ENT was founded by Jazna Stannard, Brandon Davis, and Becky Gloff.

  • Fall 2014

    Development Program

  • Spring 2015

    Alpha Pledge Class

    The first pledge class was inducted into ENT.
